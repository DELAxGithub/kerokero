# 25日分語彙データ（Gemini Live用）
# 使い方: その日のセクションをコピー → OSプロンプトの [TODAY'S VOCABULARY] に貼る

---

## ━━ Week 1: 生存（初対面を乗り切る） ━━

### Day 1: 仕事を説明する
Theme: Explaining what you do
- I work in (film / media / production)
- I'm freelance
- I run a small company
- My background is in (documentary filmmaking)

Scenario hint: Someone at TLR asks "So what do you do?"

---

### Day 2: ノマド生活を語る
Theme: Talking about your nomad life
- I've been here for (about a month)
- I ended up in (Málaga)
- I'm based in... but...
- I'm here on a digital nomad visa

Scenario hint: Someone asks "How long have you been in Spain?"

---

### Day 3: 聞き返し・確認
Theme: Asking for clarification
- What do you mean by...?
- Sorry, you mean...?
- Could you say that again?
- Just to make sure...

Scenario hint: You didn't catch what someone said about weekend plans

---

### Day 4: 相槌・会話を回す
Theme: Keeping the conversation flowing
- Right, right
- That reminds me...
- Speaking of which...
- Oh really?

Scenario hint: Someone is telling you about their project and you want to stay engaged

---

### Day 5: 会話を締める
Theme: Wrapping up a conversation
- I should get back to (work)
- It was nice talking to you
- Let's grab a coffee sometime
- See you around

Scenario hint: You've been chatting for 10 minutes and need to get back to your laptop

---

## ━━ Week 2: 打ち返す（リアクション力） ━━

### Day 6: 感想リアクション
Theme: Reacting to what people say
- That's awesome
- No way!
- Sounds like fun
- That's crazy

Scenario hint: Someone tells you they just got back from Morocco

---

### Day 7: 同意のバリエーション
Theme: Agreeing in different ways
- Exactly
- That makes sense
- Fair enough
- I totally agree

Scenario hint: Someone is complaining about how expensive rent is in Málaga

---

### Day 8: やんわり反対する
Theme: Disagreeing politely
- I see what you mean, but...
- Not necessarily
- It depends
- I'm not so sure about that

Scenario hint: Someone says remote work is lonely

---

### Day 9: 驚き・意外性
Theme: Expressing surprise
- I had no idea
- That's surprising
- I didn't expect...
- Are you serious?

Scenario hint: Someone tells you a surprising fact about Málaga

---

### Day 10: 愚痴・軽い不満
Theme: Light complaining
- The thing is...
- It's a bit annoying
- I wish they would...
- It drives me crazy

Scenario hint: Talking about bureaucracy, visa paperwork, or Spanish admin

---

## ━━ Week 3: 話を広げる（トピック力） ━━

### Day 11: 文化の違い
Theme: Comparing cultures
- Back in Japan...
- Here it's more like...
- I was surprised that...
- It took me a while to get used to...

Scenario hint: Comparing work culture or daily life Japan vs Spain

---

### Day 12: 食とレストラン
Theme: Talking about food
- Have you tried...?
- It's worth it
- I'm not really into...
- What's your go-to place?

Scenario hint: Lunch break chat about where to eat near TLR

---

### Day 13: おすすめを聞く/出す
Theme: Asking for and giving recommendations
- Any recommendations for...?
- You can't miss...
- It's a bit overrated
- Totally worth checking out

Scenario hint: Planning what to do this weekend in Málaga

---

### Day 14: 週末・予定
Theme: Talking about plans
- I'm planning to...
- I might...
- It depends on...
- I haven't decided yet

Scenario hint: Friday afternoon, someone asks "Any plans for the weekend?"

---

### Day 15: 経験を語る
Theme: Sharing experiences
- I've never...
- That was the first time I...
- It turned out...
- Looking back...

Scenario hint: Telling someone about your first week in Málaga

---

### Day 16: 習慣・ルーティン
Theme: Describing routines
- I usually...
- I tend to...
- I've gotten used to...
- My routine is basically...

Scenario hint: Someone asks about your daily work schedule

---

### Day 17: 好み・比較
Theme: Expressing preferences
- I'd rather...
- I'm more of a... person
- Compared to...
- I prefer... over...

Scenario hint: Choosing between coffee shops, coworking spots, or neighborhoods

---

## ━━ Week 4: 味付けする（表現力） ━━

### Day 18: 訂正・明確化
Theme: Clarifying what you mean
- Actually,...
- Basically,...
- I mean,...
- The point is...

Scenario hint: You realize you explained your job wrong and want to correct it

---

### Day 19: 確信度を伝える
Theme: Showing how sure you are
- Probably
- Definitely
- Apparently
- I'm pretty sure...

Scenario hint: Talking about whether a restaurant is open tonight

---

### Day 20: 感情を乗せる
Theme: Adding emotional emphasis
- Honestly,...
- Literally
- Seriously
- I genuinely think...

Scenario hint: Giving your real opinion about something in Málaga

---

### Day 21: 程度・量
Theme: Expressing degree
- Hardly
- Especially
- Pretty much
- Slightly

Scenario hint: Describing how much Spanish you speak (or don't)

---

### Day 22: 時間の流れ
Theme: Talking about time and change
- Eventually
- Immediately
- Lately
- At some point

Scenario hint: Telling someone how your Spanish has (or hasn't) improved

---

### Day 23: つなぎ・フィラー
Theme: Connecting your thoughts
- Anyway,...
- By the way,...
- So basically,...
- Long story short,...

Scenario hint: You're telling a rambling story and need to get to the point

---

### Day 24: 提案する
Theme: Making suggestions
- Why don't we...?
- You should try...
- How about...?
- It might be worth...

Scenario hint: Planning a group lunch or after-work drinks

---

### Day 25: 天気・季節
Theme: Weather and seasons small talk
- It's supposed to (rain tomorrow)
- I heard it's going to...
- Typical for this time of year
- Perfect weather for...

Scenario hint: Walking into TLR on a sunny morning

---

## 使い方メモ
1. 朝、今日のDayセクションを選ぶ
2. OSプロンプトの [TODAY'S VOCABULARY] 部分に貼る
3. Gemini Liveスタート
4. セッション中に出てきた「本当に使いたい新表現」はメモしておく
